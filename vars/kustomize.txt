<dl>
    <dt>
        <code>kustomize(path:String):String</code>
    </dt>
    <dd>
        <p>
            Generates the customized configuration per contents of a <code>kustomization.yaml</code>.
        </p>
        <ul>
            <li>
                <b>path</b> (required) - Defines the directory containing the <code>kustomization.yaml</code>.
            </li>
        </ul>
        <p style="margin-bottom: 0em;">
            Example:
        </p>
        <pre style="margin-top: 0em; margin-left: 2em; color:#657383;"><code>
kustomize(path: 'openshift/postgresql/overlays/dev')
        </code></pre>
        <p>
            For further information see <a href="https://github.com/kubernetes-sigs/kustomize">kustomize documentation</a>.
        </p>
    </dd>
</dl>
